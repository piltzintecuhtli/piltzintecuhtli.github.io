Why Piltzintecuhtli?

My go-to username online is Piltzintecuhtli. The story behind this started during a game jam meeting, where people started referring to me as the mushroom god after I drew a cute little mushroom (see below). As a natural result of being told this, I needed to know the name of the Aztec god of mushrooms, or if such a god even existed. With a quick Google search, I found Piltzintecuhtli. He was, among other things, the good of hallucinatory plants, which included mushrooms (thanks, Wikipeida)!
